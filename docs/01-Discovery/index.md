# 01 Discovery - Shiran Gilad Architecture & Interior Design

## Project Overview
Shiran Gilad is a professional architect and interior designer. This website serves as her primary portfolio, brand presence, and lead generation tool. It targets high-end residential clients looking for bespoke architectural planning and interior design services.

## Core Vision
To create a digital experience that reflects Shiran's architectural style: clean, precise, and high-end. The website must showcase her projects with high-quality imagery, explain her unique design process, and provide a cost estimation tool to qualify leads.

## Target Audience
1.  **Homeowners:** Individuals planning to build or renovate luxury apartments, penthouses, or private houses.
2.  **Real Estate Developers:** Professionals looking for an architect to partner with on high-end residential projects.

## Project Phases
- **Foundation:** Monorepo setup, backend architecture (Prisma/Postgres), and frontend framework (React 18).
- **Core Features:** Home, Process, Projects (Filterable), and Testimonials.
- **Lead Generation:** Contact forms and a custom Interior Design Cost Calculator.
- **Admin Suite:** Full CRUD management for projects, categories, testimonials, and leads.
- **Final Polish:** Image optimization, SEO, and deployment to Render/Netlify.

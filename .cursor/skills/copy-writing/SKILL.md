# Copywriting & Sales Excellence

This skill provides expert guidance and frameworks for creating high-conversion sales copy, marketing materials, and business communications.

## 1. Core Frameworks

### PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution)
Use this for landing pages, emails, and ads.
1.  **Problem**: Identify a specific pain point the customer faces.
2.  **Agitation**: Explain the emotional or financial cost of leaving the problem unsolved.
3.  **Solution**: Introduce your product/service as the relief.

### BAB (Before-After-Bridge)
Use this for testimonials and case studies.
1.  **Before**: Describe the user's world before your solution.
2.  **After**: Describe the "Transformation"—the improved state after using your solution.
3.  **Bridge**: Explain how your product made that transformation possible.

### The "So What?" Test
Every time you list a feature, ask "So what?" to find the benefit.
*   **Feature**: "10ms response time."
*   **Benefit**: "Stop losing customers to slow loading screens."

## 2. Writing Principles for 2024-2025

### Radical Specificity
Replace vague adjectives with hard data.
*   **Bad**: "We have fast shipping."
*   **Good**: "Delivered to your door in 48 hours or less."

### Skimmable Formatting
Most readers scan in an F-pattern.
*   **Bold** the most important sentence in every paragraph.
*   Use bulleted lists for benefits.
*   Ensure subheadlines tell the whole story even if the body text is ignored.

### Low-Friction CTAs
Early-stage leads are scared of "Buy Now." Use "soft" CTAs:
*   "Check availability"
*   "See how it works"
*   "Get the blueprint"

### Human-Polished Voice
Avoid "AI-isms" like *delve, unlock, comprehensive, tapestry, meticulous*. Use rhythmic variety: short punchy sentences followed by longer descriptive ones.

## 3. SEO & GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
To rank in AI-driven search (SGE):
*   Provide **Information Gain**: Add unique data or first-person experience.
*   Answer "People Also Ask" questions directly and authoritatively.

## 4. Verification Checklist
- [ ] Does the hook stop the scroll in <2 seconds?
- [ ] Is it focused on **Transformation** instead of just Features?
- [ ] Are all generic adjectives replaced with **Specific Data**?
- [ ] Is it formatted for **Skimmers** (bolding, bullets)?
- [ ] Does the CTA feel **Low-Friction**?
